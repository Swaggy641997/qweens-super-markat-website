Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for the loyalty program? ▲
You can sign up for our loyalty program in-store at any checkout counter, or online through your account settings. It's completely free to join and you'll earn 100 bonus points just for signing up!
How do I check my points balance? ▼
You can check your points balance anytime by logging into your account on our website or mobile app.
Can I share my points with family members? ▼
Points are non-transferable and can only be used by the account holder who earned them.
Do my points expire? ▼
Your points will never expire as long as you make at least one purchase every 12 months.
How do I redeem my points? ▼
You can redeem your points at checkout by selecting from available rewards in your account.
